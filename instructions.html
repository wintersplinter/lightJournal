Quick start

  1. Open LightJournal.
  2. Connect Google Drive.
  3. Enter your passphrase to open your journal.
  4. Write a note and press Save.

Notes

  • Your journal is stored in your own Google Drive (app data).
  • Your passphrase is required to decrypt entries.
  • If you use a new passphrase, you’ll get a new journal.
